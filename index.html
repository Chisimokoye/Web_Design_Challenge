SUMMARY: LATITUDE VS. X

The purpose of the project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer the equator. To accomplish this analysis, a list of over 500 cities contained in the Cities_csv was used.

After assembling the dataset Matplotlib was used to plot various information of the weather vs. latitude.
